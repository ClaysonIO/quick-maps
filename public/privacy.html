Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 4 Feb 2024

Thank you for using the Visit Tracker ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your data. By using the App, you consent to the practices described below.

Data Collection and Usage:

Google Sheets: The App stores data in a Google Sheet controlled by you. We do not access, use, or share your Google Sheets data.

Geocoding: The App sends addresses to Radar.com for geocoding purposes. Radar.com's privacy policy governs this process, and we do not store or share this data.

Your Control:

Google Sheets: You have full control over your Google Sheets data, including deletion and access permissions.

Radar.com: You can review Radar.com's privacy policy for information on how they handle geocoding data.

Data Security:

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but please note that no method of data transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes to Privacy Policy:

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. Check for updates regularly.

Contact Us:

If you have questions or concerns regarding your privacy, please contact us at daniel@clayson.io.

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. Please read it carefully, and if you do not agree, refrain from using the App.

Thank you for your trust in us.
