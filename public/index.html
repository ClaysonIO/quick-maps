Visit Tracker

A free mapping tool to make it easier for you to put addresses on a map, and work with other mappers.

All of your data is kept in your own Google Sheet, and this tool works primarily as a custom user interface to your data.

Get Started

Features of the Tool

Automatic Geocoding

You put in addresses, and we put them on the map. We use the free tier of https://www.radar.com to provide this service 

Visit Status

Track the results of your visits, so that you know who 

Your Data

All of your data is kept in a Google Sheet that you own. Share the sheet with collaborators to work with others

Privacy Policy

Your data is your own. All application data is stored in a Google Sheet that's controlled by you.
